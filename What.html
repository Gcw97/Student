What is Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a form of capitol that can be exchanged for goods and services online. Cryptocurrencies have been issued by many different companies in order to be used for the goods and services that company provides. You must first exchange physical currency for cryptocurrency in order to access the goods or services. Cryptocurrencies uses blockchain technology in order to work. This technology is appealing due to it's security advantages. Blockchain manages records and transactions using decentralized technology spread across many different computers.
